## Full Description

Back to Safety helps caregivers prepare for wandering emergencies with clear, practical tools that keep critical information close at hand.

When every minute matters, the app provides a structured response flow so you can focus on action, communication, and safety.

Key features:

- Guided emergency search flow with timer and step-by-step checklist
- Quick access to emergency profile details for responder handoff
- Emergency contact and destination organization
- On-device storage for profile and safety information
- Fast access to SMS handoff for your support circle

Built for caregivers and families supporting people at risk of wandering.

Privacy and data use:

- No account required
- Core personal data is stored locally on your device
- Permissions are requested only when needed for app features (such as contacts, camera, photos, and SMS handoff)

Privacy Policy: https://backtosafety.app/privacy.html
Support: support@backtosafety.app
