# Privacy Policy URL Setup

Use this file as the handoff checklist for publishing your privacy policy URL before store submission.

## Target URL

- Production URL: `https://backtosafety.app/privacy`

## Publish Steps

1. Host `docs/privacy-policy.md` content at the production URL above.
2. Verify the page is publicly accessible in an incognito browser.
3. Confirm no authentication is required to view the policy.
4. Paste the URL into:
   - App Store Connect -> App Privacy / Privacy Policy URL
   - Google Play Console -> App content -> Privacy Policy

## Pre-Submission Validation

- [ ] URL resolves with HTTPS
- [ ] Policy title includes "Back to Safety Privacy Policy"
- [ ] Effective date is visible
- [ ] Contact email/support info is present
