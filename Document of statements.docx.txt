Short Description – An app to guide you on the documents, steps, and timing to bring someone who might wander – back to safety.


Full description – An application which provides templates to help the user prepare documents in advance and take steps to prevent wandering.  In case someone wanders the app also includes ordered steps to take to locate the missing individual. The app also includes an important timer and scripts that can be used to work with others in locating the missing individual.  When minutes count, this app can help you maintain focus and hopefully bring the wandered individual – Back to Safety.
